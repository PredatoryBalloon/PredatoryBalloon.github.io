> Slightly Faster Wing Turning

 There is a way to turn with wings slightly faster than normal. If you roll in the same direction of your turn, your turn will actually end up being tighter. In most cases, you aren't really faced with situation where you would even need to turn that much that fast, but it is something to keep in mind.
