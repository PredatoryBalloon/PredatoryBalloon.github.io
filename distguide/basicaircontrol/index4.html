> Thruster Resets

 Remember how the max power of grip resets when at least 1 wheel touches a surface? (If not, go back to the first section and read "Grip Power Rampdown"). Well, a thruster reset is done by touching a wheel on a surface while gripflying in order to get more power to grip and thrusters while gripflying. It is used to save time and to go faster, but is really precise, and takes practice to learn how to do with any substantial degree of consistency.
