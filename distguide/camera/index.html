> Camera Reset

 There is a camera reset button. It is very useful when the camera is looking in a direction you don't want it to. It always resets the camera to look in the direction the car is pointing in. It is especially helpful in situations where you are turning around slowly, since the camera usually doesn't like to turn around to face the direction you want when you do that.
