> First Person Benefits

 If you ever want to look around more, going into first person camera helps a lot! In first person, it is a lot easier to look upwards, which is a direction the camera usually doesn't like to look in while in third person. Also, if one of your wings has been cut off by a laser, if you go into first person while flying, the car won't automatically spin constantly like it normally would when a wing gets cut off.
