You are a rock.docx
You are a rock. You have always been a rock, and you aren’t really sure when you started being a rock. However, you are not where you want to be! From what you can tell, you are stuck on a really tall cylinder of some kind. The only reason you aren’t content with this state of existence is because another, similarly tall cylinder seems to be descending from above, precisely onto the cylinder on which you are balanced. Precisely onto you, that is. You don’t know how it feels to be crushed into dust, but you also don’t intend to stick around to find out. Nudge yourself over to the ledge and fall out of the way. While you don’t know exactly how far down the fall would be if you fell, you think it still would yield better chances for your survival than if you stayed. Unfortunately, you do not have the power to MOVE. Your abilities include WAITING, THINKING, and BEING A ROCK. You also have the power of SELF-TELEPORTATION, though it often has STRANGE SIDE EFFECTS. Teleport yourself out of the way. Seeing as teleportation as the only reasonable option, you teleport yourself out of the way of the descending cylinder just before it turns you into sediment.Also, as a result of using SELF-TELEPORTATION, you are now a lion. And as a result of GRAVITY, you are now falling. Panic. Panic? Nah. You are a lion. King of the savanna. In the day, your mane glows with the incandescence of one thousand blistering golden flames. At night, your stealth is so imperceptible that you make invisible superheros look like wannabes. Your roar has the power to shatter the very substance of courage itself in all living/nonliving things within 20 kilometers, and your stare has been alleged to pierce the soul of even those thought to be without one. It won’t be long, however, before you hit the ground. You think its time you saved yourself from certain doom. Keep teleporting above yourself so you don’t fall. That won’t work. Being a lion means you can’t self-teleport. You may also want to know that as a lion, you have the power to shoot an incredibly powerful LASER out of your left eye, can activate and deactivate X-RAY VISION in your right eye, and can ROAR really really loudly. Using the LASEREYE, however, will have STRANGE SIDE EFFECTS. You don’t actually think there is anything strange about it though, considering it will probably just turn you into something else with different abilities like last time. Roar so loudly that the soundwaves bouncing off the ground will break your fall. No. Instead of doing that, you immediately activate your laser and aim it below. You manage to create a huge hole below you in what looks to be like a huge support for the tall cylinder, which should give you some extra seconds to figure out how to stop falling so fast before you hit the ground.
